KHOA NGUYEN

ABOUT

Khoa Nguyen was born in 1991 in Saigon, Vietnam and moved to Charlotte, North Carolina in 2005. Nguyen received his Bachelors of Fine Arts in Digital Media at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte in December 2017. One of his works is the laser-cut wood sculptures, which were based on his feeling and experience as an immigrant, were exhibited in his college’s BFA show. Nguyen has been included in notable group exhibitions including “Free For All Exhibition” at Hartwitzen Gallery and Studios, “2017 Juried Student Art Showcase” for Spring and Fall. He currently lives and works in Charlotte, North Carolina.
